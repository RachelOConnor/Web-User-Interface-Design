About Me

My name is Rachel O' Connor and I'm a 2nd year studying Computing in Interactive Digital Art and Design (we call ourselves IDADs!). I'm currently doing my studies in SETU Carlow.

I have a passion for 2D pixel art and have just started 3D modelling. I also love to make colourful websites (can you tell?) along with coding the occasional game or two.

Just recently, my wonderful team and I entered our game “Bronze Age Brawl” into Games Fleadh 2024 and we won an award for “Best in Game Play”! I was delighted for myself and my team.

When I do have some spare time and I'm feeling energised, I like to bake sweet treats or crochet with new patterns. I'm always looking for new recipes and patterns to try, so don't be afraid to send some my way!

On my lazy days, you'll find me on the couch watching films or on the Playstation.
